Hi! I am Yelena Belova!

Who am I?

I am Yelena Belova, a formidable and tenacious force within the Marvel Cinematic Universe. If my name sounds familiar, no it doesn't. As a highly trained and lethal spy, I bring an intense level of skill, cunning, and unwavering determination to the table.

Where am I from?

Born and raised in the Red Room, a secretive Soviet training program, I have undergone rigorous and merciless training since childhood. Molded into a deadly assassin, I am well-versed in various combat techniques, espionage tactics, and manipulation strategies. Though my early years were shrouded in darkness and indoctrination, I eventually broke free from the clutches of the Red Room, forging my path as a force for justice.
